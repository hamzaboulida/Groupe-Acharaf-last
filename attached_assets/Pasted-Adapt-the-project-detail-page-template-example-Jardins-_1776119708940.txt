Adapt the **project detail page template** (example: “Jardins de Bouskoura”) to the **WHITE luxury version** of the website.

Do NOT redesign the structure or content logic of the page.
Keep all the existing sections, but convert the full page to the same white/light premium design system used in the white version of the website.

## Objective

Make every project detail page feel:

* bright
* premium
* elegant
* highly readable
* consistent with the white version

Not dark, not mixed, and not partially inherited from the dark version.

---

## 1. Background system

Replace all dark backgrounds, dark blue blocks, and dark overlays with a light system:

* Main page background: **#FFFFFF**
* Alternate sections: **#F7F7F7** or very soft **#DCE0E7**
* Remove heavy dark gradients
* Keep subtle depth only with very light shadows, borders, and section separation

---

## 2. Text colors

Use a strict readable hierarchy:

* Main titles / headings: **#082634**
* Paragraphs / body text: **#3B5661** or similar dark readable tone
* Labels / metadata / secondary text: **#5C7480**
* Never use light grey text on white or light grey backgrounds

The whole page must have strong contrast and elegant readability.

---

## 3. Hero section

Refine the project page hero in light version:

* Keep the hero image
* Reduce or remove dark overlay
* Use a very subtle light-to-transparent overlay only if needed for readability
* Keep title, marque, location, and tagline highly readable
* Ensure CTAs are visible and premium

### CTA styles

* Primary CTA: filled **#082634** with white text
* Secondary CTA: outline **#082634** with dark text

---

## 4. Information bar / key details

For the section with:

* localisation
* type
* statut
* surfaces
* prix de départ

Convert it to a bright luxury card or light section:

* white or soft grey background
* subtle border or soft shadow
* dark text hierarchy
* strong readability

### Very important:

Highlight the **price** clearly:

* “À partir de 780.000 DH” must stand out
* use larger size and stronger weight
* keep it premium, not flashy

---

## 5. Content sections

Apply the white version styling to all sections:

* Le projet
* Galerie
* Prestations
* Art de vivre
* Emplacement
* Tarification
* Contact direct
* Projets similaires
* Newsletter

### Rules

* white / soft light backgrounds
* dark readable text
* elegant spacing
* subtle borders / shadows instead of dark blocks

---

## 6. Cards and gallery

For gallery thumbnails, similar projects, feature cards, and pricing/contact blocks:

* card background: white
* subtle shadow:
  `box-shadow: 0 10px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`
* border radius: 12px to 16px
* hover:

  * slight lift
  * soft shadow increase
  * subtle image zoom

---

## 7. Contact block

The “Intéressé par ce projet ?” block must also match the white version:

* white or very soft grey background
* dark text
* premium contrast
* WhatsApp button visible and elegant
* no dark heavy background

---

## 8. Similar projects section

The “Découvrez aussi / Projets similaires” section must match the light version:

* white / soft grey section
* clean project cards
* dark text
* visible prices
* no dark inherited styling

---

## 9. Footer + newsletter consistency

Ensure the newsletter block and footer also match the white version styling and are not inherited from dark theme styles.

---

## 10. Final consistency check

Apply the white luxury system to the ENTIRE project detail page template, so all project detail pages automatically inherit the light version.

This must include:

* background colors
* text colors
* cards
* CTAs
* forms
* pricing sections
* related projects
* footer elements

## Final goal

The project detail page must feel like:

* a luxury brochure
* a premium white editorial experience
* highly readable
* fully consistent with the white version

Not:

* a dark page inside a white site
* mixed theme inheritance
* low contrast design
