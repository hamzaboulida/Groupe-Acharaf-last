Do a full **contrast optimization audit** for the entire WHITE version, section by section.

The current issue is still: **muted grey text on grey/light backgrounds** (example: “Notre impact en chiffres” and the labels under the metrics). Fix this everywhere consistently.

## Core objective

* Make every text element readable in all sections
* Keep luxury + authority, but prioritize readability
* Don’t redesign the layout—fix the design system and component styles

---

## Create adaptive text tokens (mandatory)

Define and apply the same tokens across all components:

### On light / grey backgrounds (most of this site)

* `textPrimaryLightBg`: **#082634**
* `textMutedLightBg`: dark tone like **#3B5661** (still readable)
* `textLabelLightBg`: subtle but readable like **#5C7480**

### On dark backgrounds (hero overlays if any)

* `textPrimaryDarkBg`: white
* `textMutedDarkBg`: #BFD3DC (or similar)

Rule: muted on light must STILL be readable (no ultra-faint grey).

Audit and fix these components specifically:

### 1) Stats / metrics section (“Notre impact en chiffres”)

* Title + labels must use `textMutedLightBg` (not ultra light)

### 5) Footer

* Ensure footer text meets contrast on whatever background is used

## Minimum contrast rule

Enforce a hard rule:

* Body + labels on light backgrounds must meet **4.5:1** contrast (AA)
* If a “decorative” style cannot meet this, swap to primary text color

---

## Safety net

If any section still has poor contrast:

* either darken text (preferred)
* or lighten the background slightly
* but do NOT leave “grey on grey”

---

## Final goal

No section should have text that is hard to read. The site should feel:

* clean white
* premium
* highly readable
* consistent

This fix must be applied to ALL sections and components, not just the hero.
