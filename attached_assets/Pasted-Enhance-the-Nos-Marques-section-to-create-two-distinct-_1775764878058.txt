Enhance the “Nos Marques” section to create two distinct emotional and visual experiences for each brand.

The goal is not just visual difference, but **two completely different atmospheres**.

---

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE

Transform this section into an immersive experience where users feel:

* Estya = luxury, exclusivity, prestige
* Acharaf Immobilier = accessibility, lifestyle, comfort

---

## 🏛️ ESTYA — LUXURY EXPERIENCE

### Visual direction:

* Use darker tones:

  * #082634
  * #080629
* Add subtle dark gradient overlay
* Use night or sunset architecture visuals
* Reduce brightness slightly for a cinematic feel

### Motion:

* Slow zoom on image
* Smooth fade transitions
* Calm and elegant movement

### Typography:

* Large, minimal, high-end
* Less text, more impact

### Interaction:

* On hover:

  * deepen background
  * increase contrast
  * subtle zoom

---

## 🏡 ACHARAF IMMOBILIER — HUMAN EXPERIENCE

### Visual direction:

* Use lighter tones:

  * #DCE0E7
  * #B2BED0
* Use daylight images
* Show lifestyle, living spaces, warmth

### Motion:

* Slightly more dynamic than Estya
* Gentle movement (not too slow, not too fast)

### Typography:

* Slightly more content than Estya
* Still clean but more accessible

### Interaction:

* On hover:

  * brighten slightly
  * soft overlay
  * smooth zoom

---

## 🔄 TRANSITION BETWEEN BOTH

### IMPORTANT:

The transition between Estya and Acharaf must feel like:
👉 switching between two worlds

### Add:

* smooth visual transition between both blocks
* slight shift in tone when moving cursor from one to the other
* optional subtle center divider animation

---

## 🎨 DESIGN RULES

* Keep global coherence with main brand (#082634)
* Do not break overall identity
* Differences must feel intentional, not random

---

## 🎯 FINAL RESULT

User should feel:

When seeing Estya:
👉 “This is luxury. Exclusive. Premium.”

When seeing Acharaf:
👉 “This is accessible, comfortable, real life.”

Not:
❌ same feeling with different text
❌ simple cards
❌ only color variation

But:
✅ two distinct brand universes
