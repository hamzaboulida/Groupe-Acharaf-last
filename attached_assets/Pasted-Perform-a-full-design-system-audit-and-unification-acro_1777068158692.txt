Perform a **full design system audit and unification across the entire website**.

## Scope

Check **ALL pages and ALL sections**, including:

* Home
* À propos
* Nos marques
* Projets (listing + detail pages)
* Actualités (listing + article pages)
* Contact
* Carrières
* Footer, header, and all components

---

## Objective

Ensure the website is **100% consistent, premium, and aligned with one unified design system**.

---

## What to audit and fix

### 1. Colors

* Use ONLY official brand colors
* Remove any inconsistent or random colors
* Ensure:

  * Primary color is consistent everywhere
  * Secondary colors are used properly
  * Background colors are harmonized
* Fix contrast issues (text always readable)

---

### 2. Typography

* Unify:

  * Font families
  * Font weights
  * Font sizes
  * Line heights
  * Letter spacing
* Ensure consistent hierarchy:

  * H1, H2, H3, body text, captions
* Fix any sections where text looks different or off-brand

---

### 3. Buttons & CTAs

* Standardize all buttons:

  * Same style (primary / secondary)
  * Same border radius
  * Same hover effects
* Ensure consistency across:

  * Hero CTAs
  * Forms
  * Cards
  * Navigation

---

### 4. Spacing & Layout

* Normalize spacing:

  * Section padding
  * Margins between elements
* Ensure consistent grid and alignment
* Remove any visual imbalance

---

### 5. Components consistency

Unify all reusable elements:

* Cards (projects, articles, jobs)
* Forms
* Inputs
* Filters
* Navigation menu
* Footer

Everything should follow the same visual logic

---

### 6. Hero sections

* Ensure all hero sections follow the same design language:

  * Overlay style
  * Typography placement
  * Spacing
* Consistent premium look across all pages

---

### 7. Animations & interactions

* Standardize:

  * Hover effects
  * Transitions
  * Micro-interactions
* Keep them smooth and subtle (luxury feel)

---

## Important

* Do NOT change content or structure
* Only improve consistency, harmony, and design quality
* Keep the luxury / premium positioning

---

## Final goal

A website that feels:

* Cohesive
* Elegant
* Premium
* Designed as ONE system (not separate pages)

Everything must look like it belongs to the same high-end brand.
