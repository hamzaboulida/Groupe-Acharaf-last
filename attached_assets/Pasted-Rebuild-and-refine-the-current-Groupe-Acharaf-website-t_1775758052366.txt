### Creative direction

The website must feel like:

* **Ultra-premium**
* **Cinematic**
* **Smooth and immersive**
* **Architectural and artistic**
* A mix of **luxury real estate + fashion house + digital experience**

### Important visual correction

The current website feels **too light** because of the white/light backgrounds across many sections.
Refactor the design system to make it more premium by using the brand palette more strategically:

#### Main brand colors

* Primary: **#082634**
* Secondary: **#8EA4AF**
* Tertiary: **#DCE0E7**

#### Additional secondary colors

* **#B2BED0**
* **#D1E8F8**
* **#080629**
* **#212F4F**

### Color system to apply

* Use **dark, rich backgrounds** for most of the experience
* Make **#082634** and **#080629** the dominant foundation colors
* Use **#212F4F** for depth, overlays, panels, and transitions
* Use **#DCE0E7** and **#B2BED0** only for contrast, breathing space, and selected lighter sections
* Avoid large flat white sections
* Remove any background colors that break the luxury cinematic mood

### Typography

* Use **Quiche Text** for hero titles and major headings
* Use **Roboto** for body text and UI text
* Increase elegance through spacing, scale, and composition
* Keep typography editorial and refined
