Redesign the **“Nos Marques”** page so it starts with **only ONE section** that shows the 2 brands (Estya + Acharaf Immobilier) with an experience similar to the animated brand chooser on the homepage.

Do NOT remove the homepage style system. Keep the luxury vibe, transitions, and premium typography. Fix only this page’s flow.

---

## UX requirement (core)

* On initial load, show only:

  * Estya
  * Acharaf Immobilier
  * both as an immersive animated split experience (like the homepage “Nos Marques” section)
* When the user selects/clicks one brand:

  * keep the top brand section visible and keep content readable at all times
  * reveal the brand-specific sections below it
  * hide/slide away the other brand’s sections (or fade them out)
* Switching brands should instantly switch the revealed content below (without leaving the page)

---

## Brand selector behavior

* Brand cards must be fully visible (titles + descriptions + CTAs always readable)
* Hover should enhance, not reveal text
* Selection must:

  * visually “activate” the chosen brand
  * dim/deactivate the other side elegantly
* Add smooth animation (fade/slide/scale) on transitions
* Mobile must work perfectly (no reliance on hover)

---

## Brand content (below the selector)

Each brand’s revealed content should include:

* Brand headline + positioning tagline
* Short brand story section
* Key highlights grid (4–6 items)
* “Projet phares” preview (2–4 projects for that brand)
* CTA block: “Découvrir les projets” / “Prendre contact”

This content must exist for BOTH brands, but only one is shown at a time.

---

## State + URL (important)

* Keep the selected brand in state
* Optional but ideal: reflect selection in URL:

  * /nos-marques?brand=estya or #estya
    so a direct link opens the page with the right brand already selected
* Back button must behave naturally

---

## SEO

* Keep both brand contents in the DOM so search engines can still see the content (even if visually hidden with CSS)
* Maintain proper headings hierarchy

---

## Final goal

Make **Nos Marques** feel like a single, cinematic, interactive gateway:

* first choose the universe
* then the page “unfolds” the story for the chosen brand
